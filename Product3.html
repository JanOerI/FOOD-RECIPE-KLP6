Sosis Solo

Ingredients:

For the skin:

  • 300 grams all-purpose flour (medium/high protein)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground pepper
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 700 ml water
  • 3 tbsp margarine, melted

For the filling:

  • 500 grams ground beef (or boiled chicken, shredded and ground)
  • 5 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 stalk green onion, thinly sliced
  • 1.5 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp ground pepper
  • 1/4 tsp ground coriander
  • 100 ml water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil for sautéing

Prepare the filling:

  1. Sauté the shallots and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the ground meat and stir until it changes color.
  3. Pour in the water.
  4. Add the salt, sugar, ground pepper, and ground coriander. Stir well.
  5. Cook until the water evaporates, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the green onions, cook for a while, then turn off the heat.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  8. Let it cool.

Prepare the skin:

  1. Combine the flour, salt, ground pepper, and sugar. Mix well.
  2. Add the eggs and gradually pour in the water, stirring with a hand whisk until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture to remove any lumps.
  4. Add the melted margarine and mix well.

Cooking the skin:

  1. Heat a non-stick flat pan and lightly grease it with margarine.
  2. Pour 1 ladle of batter into the pan and swirl it to spread evenly.
  3. Cook until set and repeat until all the batter is used up.

Assembling the rolls:

  1. Take one piece of the skin, add the meat filling, then fold and roll it up.
  2. Repeat until all the skins and filling are used.

Frying:

  1. Heat oil for frying.
  2. Dip the rolled solo sausages into the beaten egg, then fry until golden brown.
  3. Remove and drain.
  4. Serve with fresh chili or chili sauce.
